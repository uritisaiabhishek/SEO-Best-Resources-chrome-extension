Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our Google Chrome Extension.

Permissions

This extension requests minimal permissions to function effectively. We only request permissions that are necessary to display and redirect to website links. These include:

  • activeTab: This permission is needed to access and modify content on the current tab for redirection purposes.

We do not request any unnecessary permissions. Removing unused permissions is essential to ensure your security and privacy.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect any personal information or data from users. The links you click within the extension are purely for redirection to external websites and are not stored or monitored by us.

Google Analytics

In the future, we may implement Google Analytics to track user interactions such as clicks on website links within the extension. This will be done solely for the purpose of improving the user experience by understanding which links are most useful.

Third-Party Services

This extension contains links to third-party websites. We do not control or take responsibility for the content or privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those websites before engaging with them.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any updates.
